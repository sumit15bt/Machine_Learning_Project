                                     ANALYSE  
                                

                                

                                    

                                        Images are the easiest medium through which people can express their emotions on social networking sites. Social media users are increasingly using images and videos to express their opinions and share their experiences. Sentiment analysis of such large scale visual content can help better extract user sentiments toward events or topics, such as those in image tweets, so that prediction of sentiment from visual content is complementary to textual sentiment analysis.
